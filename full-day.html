Full Day Open Trip



Experience an unforgettable adventure with Agua Hermosa in Labuan Bajo, where you'll explore the famous Komodo Dragons and enjoy the stunning landscapes and crystal-clear waters surrounding this tropical paradise.


Book your unforgettable voyage tour today!

Pink Beach

A dreamy shoreline with rare blush-pink sand and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling and sunbathing.

Padar Island

Famous for its dramatic hilltop views of tri-colored bays, Padar offers one of Indonesia’s most iconic photo spots.

Komodo Island

Home to the legendary Komodo dragons, this island blends wild adventure with rugged natural beauty.

Manta Point

A diving and snorkeling paradise where majestic manta rays glide gracefully through the turquoise sea.

Siaba Island

Known as “Turtle City,” Siaba’s calm, shallow reefs make it a snorkeling haven for spotting gentle sea turtles.

Taka Makassar

A stunning, tiny sandbar surrounded by vibrant reefs and shallow turquoise water, perfect for drone shots and quick dips.

Sabolo Island

A peaceful gem with lush greenery and rich marine life, ideal for snorkeling or just soaking in the serenity.

Kelor Island

A small, hilly island offering a short hike to panoramic views and soft-sand beaches for quiet escapes.

Kanawa Island

A tranquil getaway with clear water, thriving coral reefs, and relaxing vibes both above and below the surface.

Bidadari Island

Often called “Angel Island,” this scenic spot is perfect for a day trip with white sands and calm waters great for swimming.
