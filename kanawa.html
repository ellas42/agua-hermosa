Kanawa Island


Kanawa Island is a serene and picturesque island located just a short boat ride from Labuan Bajo, known for its powdery white sand, turquoise waters, and rich marine life. It’s a peaceful retreat perfect for sunbathing, snorkeling, and unwinding in nature’s beauty.

When you visit Kanawa Island, you can walk straight from the beach into crystal-clear waters filled with coral gardens, starfish, and schools of tropical fish. The gentle waves and shallow reefs make it an ideal snorkeling spot for beginners and seasoned explorers alike. You might even spot baby reef sharks or sea turtles if you're lucky!

Kanawa Island offers a tranquil escape and a chance to experience the untouched charm of Indonesia’s island life, making it a must-visit for travelers exploring the wonders of Labuan Bajo and Komodo National Park.
