Kelor Island


Kelor Island is a small, scenic island near Labuan Bajo that’s known for its breathtaking panoramic views and crystal-clear waters. With its soft sandy beach and lush green hill, it’s the perfect spot for a mix of adventure and relaxation in a tropical paradise.

When you visit Kelor Island, you can hike up the hill for a stunning 360-degree view of the surrounding islands and turquoise sea — the short trek is steep but absolutely worth it. After the hike, you can cool off by snorkeling in the shallow waters, where vibrant coral reefs and colorful fish await just a few meters from the shore.

Kelor Island offers a beautiful balance of land and sea adventures, making it a favorite stop for island-hopping trips around the Komodo National Park area.
