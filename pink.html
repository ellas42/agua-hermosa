Pink Beach


One of the most beautiful and unique beaches in the world, named for its stunning pink sand.

The soft, rosy hue comes from the combination of white sand and crushed red coral fragments, creating a picturesque spot perfect for relaxation and exploration.

At Pink Beach, you can enjoy swimming in crystal-clear waters, snorkel to discover vibrant coral reefs, and relax on the beach while soaking in the tropical vibes. If you're lucky, you might even spot some local wildlife, like sea turtles or colorful fish, making your visit to Pink Beach even more unforgettable!
