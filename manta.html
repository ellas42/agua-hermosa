Manta Point


Manta Point is a famous dive and snorkeling spot known for its incredible marine life, especially the graceful manta rays. It’s one of the best places in the world to encounter these majestic creatures up close. The crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs make it a perfect destination for underwater adventures.

When you visit Manta Point, you can snorkel into the deep blue sea and swim alongside manta rays as they glide gracefully through the water. These gentle giants, with their wide wingspans, are an unforgettable sight! Besides manta rays, the area is also home to a variety of colorful fish, corals, and other marine life, making it a paradise for snorkelers and divers.

Manta Point offers an exciting and unique opportunity to connect with nature and explore the wonders of Labuan Bajo’s underwater world.
