Bidadari Island


Bidadari Island, also known as Angel Island, is a charming and peaceful getaway just minutes from Labuan Bajo. Surrounded by clear blue waters and vibrant coral reefs, itâ€™s a perfect destination for snorkeling, swimming, and soaking up the sun in a calm, tropical setting.

When you visit Bidadari Island, you can snorkel right off the beach and discover a colorful underwater world teeming with fish, corals, and other marine life. The gentle waves and warm water make it great for relaxing dips or floating under the sun. With its soft sand and quiet atmosphere, it's also ideal for a beach picnic or a romantic walk along the shore.

Bidadari Island offers a quick and dreamy escape from the mainland, making it a favorite stop for island-hopping and a hidden gem in the Labuan Bajo area.
