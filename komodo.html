Komodo Island


Primarily known for being home to the legendary Komodo Dragons. This UNESCO World Heritage Site offers a mix of stunning landscapes, unique wildlife, and crystal-clear waters, making it a top destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike.

When you visit Komodo Island, the main highlight is, of course, the chance to see the mighty Komodo Dragons in their natural habitat.

Besides spotting Komodo Dragons, you can also have chance to spot the wildlife
