Sabolo Island


Sabolo Island is a hidden gem near Labuan Bajo, known for its stunning white-sand beaches and calm, turquoise waters. It’s a peaceful escape perfect for snorkeling, swimming, or simply relaxing on a pristine sandbar surrounded by crystal-clear sea.

When you visit Sabolo Island, you can stroll along the crescent-shaped sandbar that appears during low tide and dip into the shallow waters that sparkle in the sunlight. The calm seas make it ideal for snorkeling, where you’ll spot colorful fish and small coral formations beneath the surface. It’s also a great spot to enjoy a private picnic or catch a breathtaking sunset.

Sabolo Island offers a tranquil and picturesque getaway, making it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the natural beauty of Labuan Bajo and the nearby Komodo islands.
